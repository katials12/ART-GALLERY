ART GALLERY

Baroque

Girl with a Pearl Earing, c. 1665

Girl with a Pearl Earring represents a young woman in a dark shallow space, an intimate setting that draws the viewer's attention exclusively on her.

The Ecstasy of Saint Teresa, 1647-1652.

Bernini employs imagery which suggests sensual pleasure, but only in order to convey the tangible nature of Teresa's experience - a manifestation of her love of God and her yearning for spiritual union with him.

Galerie des Glaces, Palace of Versailles.

The Hall of Mirrors is a grand Baroque style gallery and one of the most emblematic rooms in the royal Palace of Versailles near Paris, France.

The Calling of Saint Matthew

This period of art was really when artists began to perfect the art of using shadows in their work. Carvaggio captures here the perfect use of shadows to force a perspective on the viewer as if the children are looking at something.

Las Meninas

A highly celebrated work of Baroque Art, it features the figures of the Spanish Royal Family and their attendants, with the princess Margarita Theresa as its focus. The work acts similarly to a snapshot, capturing the moment of action where the ladies attend upon the young princess.
